---
title: "Intracranial Neural Recordings"
summary: "High-resolution electrophysiology to study human cognition at the single-neuron and population level."
order: 2
---

Intracranial EEG (iEEG) provides unparalleled spatial and temporal resolution for studying human brain function. We record from electrodes placed for clinical purposes, enabling us to observe neural activity during complex cognitive tasks with millisecond precision.

This approach allows us to link specific neural events — such as sharp-wave ripples or theta oscillations — to behavioral outcomes in real time.
