summary: "Carefully designed tasks that isolate memory and decision processes in controlled settings."

---

Our behavioral paradigms are designed to probe specific cognitive mechanisms — such as episodic recall, generalization, or value-based choice — while minimizing confounds. We combine online experiments with in-person studies in clinical settings.

These experiments provide the behavioral ground truth against which we interpret our neural recordings.
