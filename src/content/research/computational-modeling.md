---
title: "Computational Modeling"
summary: "Building and testing formal models of learning, memory, and decision-making in the human brain."
order: 3
---

We develop computational models that formalize hypotheses about how the brain learns from experience. These range from reinforcement learning models to neural network architectures inspired by hippocampal-cortical interactions.

By fitting models to both neural data and behavior, we can adjudicate between competing theories of memory-guided cognition.
