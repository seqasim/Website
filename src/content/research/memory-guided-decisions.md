---
title: "Memory-Guided Decision-Making"
summary: "How episodic and semantic memory systems interact to guide choices in uncertain environments."
order: 1
---

We study how memories of past experiences shape the decisions we make today. Using intracranial EEG recordings from patients performing decision tasks, we identify neural signatures of memory retrieval and evaluate how they predict upcoming choices.

Our work bridges cognitive neuroscience and reinforcement learning, asking whether the brain implements something akin to model-based planning when faced with novel situations.
