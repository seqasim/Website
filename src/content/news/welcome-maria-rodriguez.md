---
title: "Lab welcomes new postdoctoral fellow"
date: 2025-09-01
summary: "Dr. Maria Rodriguez joins the lab to study single-neuron correlates of decision confidence."
---

We are excited to welcome Dr. Maria Rodriguez as a new postdoctoral fellow in the Qasim Lab. Maria completed her PhD at MIT, where she studied population coding in prefrontal cortex. She will lead our efforts to analyze single-neuron recordings from medial temporal lobe during value-based decision tasks.
