---
title: "NIH R01 grant awarded"
date: 2025-03-01
summary: "The lab receives a five-year NIH R01 to study memory-guided decision-making in humans."
---

We are thrilled to announce that the Qasim Lab has been awarded a five-year NIH R01 grant to investigate how episodic memory guides decision-making in humans. This funding will support our intracranial recording studies and computational modeling efforts.
