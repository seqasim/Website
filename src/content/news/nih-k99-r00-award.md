---
title: "NIH K99/R00 Pathway to Independence Award"
date: 2023-09-01
summary: "Dr. Qasim awarded a K99/R00 from the National Institute of Mental Health to study decision-making and memory."
---

Dr. Qasim has received a K99/R00 Pathway to Independence Award from the National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH). The project, "The computational and neural mechanisms linking decision-making and memory in humans," supports the lab's research program on how memory guides human choices.
