Dr. Qasim has received a K99/R00 Pathway to Independence Award from the National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH). The project, "The computational and neural mechanisms linking decision-making and memory in humans," supports the lab's broader research program on the mental computations that make memories sticky and how those processes relate to mental health.
