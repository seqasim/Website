---
title: "Qasim Lab opens at Rutgers"
date: 2024-11-01
summary: "The Qasim Lab begins at Rutgers Robert Wood Johnson Medical School and the Rutgers Brain Health Institute."
---

Dr. Salman Qasim joins Rutgers Robert Wood Johnson Medical School as Assistant Professor of Neurosurgery and Core Faculty at the Rutgers Brain Health Institute. The lab investigates biomarkers of complex cognitive function using direct-brain recordings in human neurosurgical patients.
