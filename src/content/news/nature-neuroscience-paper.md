Our paper "Neural signatures of memory retrieval predict subsequent decisions" has been published in Nature Neuroscience. Using intracranial EEG, we show that hippocampal sharp-wave ripples preceding choice predict whether participants rely on episodic memory. Read the full paper at the link on our Publications page.
